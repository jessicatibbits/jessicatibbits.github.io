I am a freelance illustrator and journalist working in London. Having worked in the magazine industry for eight years, I decided to turn my lifelong passion for illustration into a career at the end of 2014.

I have worked on a broad range of creative briefs, but specialise in personal stationery, producing bespoke hand-illustrated material for weddings, christenings, press launches and corporate events.

As well as private commissions, I have produced illustrations and calligraphy for brands including Guerlain, Max Factor, Coty UK and Eileen Fisher.
