With over eight years' experience in the publishing industry, I am an established beauty and travel journalist and have worked at a variety of magazines including Cosmopolitan, Look and, most recently, InStyle, as Beauty Writer and Online Health & Fitness Editor.

From interviewing high profile celebrities including Gwyneth Paltrow, Cate Blanchett and Blake Lively to writing in-depth skincare and travel features, reporting from backstage at fashion week and directing still life shoots, I have a broad range of journalistic experience.

I have also presented videos in association with global beauty brands including Clinique, Estee Lauder and Babyliss.
